Customer 360° Support for Zoho Teams

A full–stack Zoho support assistant that connects SalesIQ, Zoho Desk, Zoho CRM, and Zoho Subscriptions. It qualifies visitors, surfaces past tickets and plan details, and routes conversations to the right engineer – all in one place.

Built with
Zoho SalesIQ · Zoho Desk · Zoho CRM · Zoho Subscriptions

Live Demo: Customer 360° Support Bot

Click the chat bubble in the bottom-right corner to talk to the bot. It will ask whether you're a New visitor or an Existing customer and then show how the full Customer 360° view works.

For new visitors, the bot collects name, email, company and requirement, scores the lead (Hot / Warm / Cold) and can push it into Zoho CRM for your sales team.

For existing customers, the bot verifies the email, fetches the customer’s latest Zoho Desk tickets and active Zoho Subscriptions plan, and then forwards the chat to an operator with that context attached.

Tip: Try the New visitor flow first, then the Existing customer flow with your email.

Why Customer 360° Support for Zoho?

1. Automatic Lead Capture

Every qualified website visitor becomes a structured lead in Zoho CRM with name, email, company, requirement and a priority score – no manual entry needed.

2. Smart Visitor Routing

The bot classifies traffic as New visitor or Existing customer, then sends them down the right flow – lead creation for sales, or rich-context handoff for support engineers.

3. 360° Context for Engineers

When a chat reaches an operator, they instantly see recent Zoho Desk tickets, subscription status from Zoho Subscriptions, and lead information from Zoho CRM – all sourced via Zoho Connections.

4. Built for Zoho’s Internal Stack

Designed specifically for Zoho teams: it uses SalesIQ Zobot, OAuth Connections, and Deluge to talk to Desk, CRM and Subscriptions, so it fits naturally into existing Zoho workflows.

How it works

  1. Visitor lands on this website and opens the Zoho SalesIQ chat bubble.
  2. Bot asks: “New visitor” or “Existing customer”.
  3. For a new visitor, the bot collects details, scores priority, and can create a Lead in Zoho CRM.
  4. For an existing customer, the bot verifies their email, fetches last tickets from Zoho Desk and plan info from Zoho Subscriptions, then forwards the conversation to an operator with all that context.
  5. After the chat, the bot can ask for a quick CSAT / rating so teams can monitor support quality.
