Welcome to Platform 360° Support – Zoho Help Hub

Elevate your customer and engineer experience with a single Zoho-powered support bot. The Customer 360° Zobot collects issues for CRM, Desk, Mail, Books, Projects and more, then routes them to the right team with full context from Zoho Desk, CRM & Subscriptions.

What can you raise issues about?

Use this hub for questions or incidents related to your Zoho products: Mail, CRM, Desk, Books, People, Projects, Payroll, Expense and other internal tools. Just open the chat in the bottom-right corner, tell the bot which product you’re using, and it will guide you through a short flow to gather all the details your engineers need.

How to contact the support bot

There is no separate form or phone number here – everything starts from the SalesIQ chat bubble. Once you start a conversation, the bot can:

• Classify your request (bug, billing, configuration, feature idea)
• Look up recent tickets and subscription plan information
• Forward the chat to the right operator queue with context attached

Please tap the chat icon on the bottom-right side to initiate a conversation with the Platform 360° Support bot.
