Customer 360° Support for Zoho

A smart Zoho SalesIQ Zobot that qualifies visitors, captures leads and pushes them into Zoho CRM – so your team gets full context.

Built with
Zoho SalesIQ + Zoho CRM

Live Demo: Customer 360° Support Bot

Open the chat bubble in the bottom-right corner to talk to the bot. It will ask whether you're a New visitor or an Existing customer, then walk you through the flow.

For new visitors, the bot collects your name, email, company and requirement, and automatically creates a Lead in Zoho CRM. For existing customers, it forwards the chat to an operator with your email so support gets instant context.

Tip: Run the New visitor flow, then open Zoho CRM > Leads to see the record created by the bot.

Why Customer 360° Support?

1. Automatic Lead Capture

Every qualified website visitor becomes a structured lead in Zoho CRM with name, email, company and requirement – no manual entry needed.

2. Smart Visitor Routing

New visitors go through a guided flow, while existing customers are routed to support with their email and context pre-attached.

3. 360° Context for Your Team

Sales and support see the same information inside Zoho CRM and SalesIQ, making it easier to follow up, prioritize and close conversations.

How it works

  1. Visitor lands on this website and opens the Zoho SalesIQ chat bubble.
  2. Bot asks: “New visitor” or “Existing customer”.
  3. New visitor → bot collects details → creates a Lead in Zoho CRM.
  4. Existing customer → bot verifies email → forwards chat to operator with context.
