Customer 360° Support for Zoho

This website doubles as a demo for your Customer 360° Zobot. Open the chat bubble in the bottom-right corner to talk to it. The bot asks if you’re a New visitor or an Existing customer, then:

1. Automatic Lead Capture (CRM)

New visitors go through a short questionnaire. The bot scores the request (Hot / Warm / Cold) and creates a Lead in Zoho CRM with name, email, company, need and priority.

2. Ticket & Plan Context (Desk + Subscriptions)

For existing customers, the bot looks up their last three Zoho Desk tickets and (optionally) their Zoho Subscriptions plan, so agents see context instantly.

3. Smart Handoff to Human Agents

When needed, the conversation is forwarded to an operator together with the collected context, so support engineers can jump in without asking repeated questions.

Tip: Try the “Existing customer” path using your own email. In your final hackathon demo you can show Zoho Desk tickets and plan info appearing on the agent side.

How the full flow works

  1. Visitor lands here and opens the Zoho SalesIQ chat bubble.
  2. Bot asks: “New visitor” or “Existing customer”.
  3. New visitor → bot collects details → creates a Lead in Zoho CRM.
  4. Existing customer → bot verifies email → fetches Desk tickets / plan → forwards to operator.
